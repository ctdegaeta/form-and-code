Project 7 Exquisite Corpse

Description

This is a drawing I made, I collaborated with two other students in which we divided the parts of our character sketches and combined them to create an 'exquisite corpse.'

Reflections

This project to me was mainly about accurately depicting my partners character depictions which I really tried my best to accomplish. I struggled with the middle body section the most, and decided it was best that i take some creative liberties in able to finish the project, I feel very confident in that I represented the lower body to perfection, and the head was alright, I got the main features across.
