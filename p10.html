Project 10 Conventional Camera

Project Outline

The goal of this project was to create a sketch that utilizes the webcam of our devices with a specific purpose in mind.

Reflections

In my sketch I first started out with a filter that produced red circles when the camera picked up on dark areas within the webcam, I thought these ressembled red blood cells and would work so serve a purpose for my design. I would like to think that my sketch may be a digital sign near a sidewalk that picks up when people pass by. They're reflection turns into the blood cells making for a fun interactivity that may convince someone to donate their blood to help others with a blood deficiency that may be in more need than them.(I cannot grant camera permission on website at the moment so you must go to p5.js to utiliize the webcam feature) .
