Project 5 Experimental Clock

Description

This is a drawing I made to decipher time in a non traditional way. The clock counts down the seconds for every month of the year and is supposed to make the viewer realize that every second in their life counts.

Reflections

The clock coding was more simpler than i anticipated which gave me more leeway in creating the aesthetics that I wanted, such as the futuristic font and orange colorway. I also wanted to focus on the affect that the clock would affect the viewer, such as a clock that persuades the viewer to not waste any of their time as there is not an infintite amount of time, like a clock that counts up may make you feel like.
